About Projectship

If you are looking for a partner to work on your personal project, then we will help you. On this website, you will be able to search partners from different fields: "Business", "Engineering" and "Computer Science".

Mission

Our mission is to make it easier for students to find the project partners by matching them with people who share smiliar interests, eventually helping them to be able to bring their ideas to the real world.
